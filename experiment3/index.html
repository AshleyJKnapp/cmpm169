Experiment 3 - Recursive Visual and Sound Generation

Description

I wanted to play around with generating sounds accompanying a recursive drawing being drawn in steps. Click to start. Refresh the page to restart.

Technical

I took a simple recursive drawing and broke it down to draw in steps. At each step, it plays a predetermined sound from a p5.Oscillator.

Reflection

I would love to extend this project into playing dynamically generated sounds as an L-system is drawn, now that this simplified version shows that it can sound and look pretty neat.
