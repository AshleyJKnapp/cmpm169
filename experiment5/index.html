Experiment 5 - Campfire Storytime

Description

Press spacebar to rotate to the next part of the story.
Sitting around a campfire, a group tells one bit of a story at a time.
Collaboration between myself, Kevin and Nate.

Technical

My group used Tracery and Phaser to create this. Each person in the group created our own grammar that would get displayed one at a time. Phaser is used to animate the image and control generating a new line of the story.

Reflection

This assignment started becoming really fun once I got my grammar in and it started generating sentences. It made me feel excited so I added a few more word variations. I also really enjoy the concept that we ended up going with, and I think Kevin's animation portrays the spinning well.
